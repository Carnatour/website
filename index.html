About

Brendan Kennedy is a composer whose music engages with memory, personality, place, and history. His music is often driven by a single emotion or snapshot in time. He has collaborated with groups such as Hub New Music and the Music Department at Gonzaga University. In addition to concert compositions, he is also working with student filmmakers to create diverse soundscapes for their projects.
